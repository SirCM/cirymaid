Princess Cut

The Diamyric Difference

Since our founding in 2010, Diamyric has been committed to offering only the finest diamonds that meet our exacting standards. We believe that true luxury is found in exceptional quality, ethical sourcing, and personalized service.

Every Diamyric diamond is certified by independent gemological laboratories and carefully selected by our expert gemologists for its exceptional qualities. We partner only with suppliers who adhere to the Kimberley Process to ensure all our diamonds are conflict-free.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our products to the relationships we build with our clients. We provide personalized consultation to help you find the perfect diamond that aligns with your vision and values.
